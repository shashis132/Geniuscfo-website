10 — FAQ

Got questions?

Do I need accounting knowledge?+
No. Connect your bank and the system builds your books for you. Ask questions in plain Hindi or English. You will never have to see a chart of accounts.
How is this different from Tally, Zoho, or QuickBooks?+
Those are ledger tools. You still need a person to run them. GeniusCFO replaces the ledger, the person running the ledger, and the spreadsheets you keep alongside it. Books and intelligence are one system.
Can I migrate from Tally?+
Yes. One-click migration. Your historical data comes over — accounts, ledgers, transactions, vouchers — and reconciles against your bank feed automatically.
Will my CA use it too?+
Yes. Persona modes give CAs full access to trial balance, journal entries, reconciliation queues, and audit trails. They work faster, not differently.
Is my data safe?+
DPDP Act compliant. Account Aggregator framework with 6 banks live in beta. SOC 2 certification in progress. Encryption in transit and at rest. Your data stays in India.
What is included in ₹9,999?+
All 16 services. Accounting, bookkeeping, financial statements, payroll, tax management, ITR filing, GST filing, virtual CFO, budgeting, expense management, invoicing, bank reconciliation, and more. One price. One platform.
